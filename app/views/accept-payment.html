You may be eligible for a recognition payment of up to £4,500

We've confirmed your identity and that you have an eligible qualification. We still need to review your employment evidence before we can make a decision.

Before you continue your claim

The payment may affect your finances. You should check how it could impact you before you decide whether to accept it. While the payment is £4,500, the amount paid into your account may be lower if you need to pay tax or repay your student loan.

Student loan deductions

If you are currently repaying a student loan, a deduction from your payment will go towards repaying it.

Income tax

If you pay the basic rate of Income Tax, we will pay tax and National Insurance for you and you will receive the full amount. You may have to pay additional Income Tax if you pay the higher rate.

Benefits

The payment may affect any benefits you receive. You should check how they might be affected before you accept the payment.
