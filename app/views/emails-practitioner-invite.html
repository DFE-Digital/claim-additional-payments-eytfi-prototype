We know how much hard work goes into supporting young children every day. Your commitment to their early learning helps to shape the next generation.

What the payment is

To recognise your work, we are offering a yearly payment of £4,500 to eligible early years teachers. You can claim this year and for the next 2 financial years.

The payment counts as taxable income. This means it may affect the tax you pay, but you will not have to pay the payment back.

How to claim

You can start your claim now.

Claim deadline
You must submit your claim by 31 March 2027.
