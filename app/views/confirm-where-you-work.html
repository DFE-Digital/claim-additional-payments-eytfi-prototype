Confirm where you work

Upload a recent document to show that you work at Sunny Days Nursery.

The document must:

  • show your full name
  • show the name of your workplace
  • be dated within the last 2 months

For example, you can upload:

  • a recent payslip
  • a letter from your employer
  • a P60

You can upload a clear photo or a scanned copy of the document.
