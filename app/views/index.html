Self serve / Ops / Admin

Prototype area for self service / Ops area back end.

  • /ops will take you to a prototype of the operations area for the service with any new concepts.
  • /ops/services_ey Problem 7, CAPT-3607, Automation control. The system does not provide operations users with control over approval automation. When payroll processes change or defects are identified, users cannot immediately pause automatic approvals.
  • /ops/claims/payroll Problem 6, CAPT-3423, HMRC retry. When HMRC bank validation fails, operations users must manually contact claimants to verify their bank details, even when the failure may be caused by a temporary issue with the HMRC service.
  • /ops/claims/employment Problem 9, 193, Employment data. The employment check task does not provide a complete view of TPS employment data in the task notes.
  • provide evidence concept will take you to a page where you can upload evidence for your claim, requested on behalf of a support agent.
