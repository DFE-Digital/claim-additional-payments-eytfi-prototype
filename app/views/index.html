Claim a recognition payment

Use this service to claim a recognition payment if you're an eligible early years teacher.

You can check if you're eligibile by visiting the guidance.

Submitting a claim takes about 10 minutes.

You will need:

  • Proof of identity - you'll need a GOV.UK One Login account if you don't already have one. This requires a UK passport, UK photo driving licence or biometric residence permit to set up.
  • Your bank details - full name on the account, sort code and account number to receive the payment.

If you have questions about eligibility, accessibility or assisted digital needs email EYTrecognitionpayment@education.gov.uk
