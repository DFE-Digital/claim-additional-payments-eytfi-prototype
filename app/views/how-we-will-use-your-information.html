How we'll use your information

Student loan deductions

We'll check if you're repaying a student loan. If you are, we'll take the right amount from your recognition payment automatically.

Income Tax

We'll check if you pay the higher rate of Income Tax. If you do, we'll take any extra tax you owe from your payment. You do not need to do anything.

To pay you

The Department of Education (DfE) will make the payment directly to you. To complete the rest of your application, you will need your:

  • home address
  • gender
  • bank account details
